How To Use Simple Custom UI Themes For Vega X:

1. Get Your Image (The Bigger The Better)
2. Open Vega
3. Drag + Drop Your Image Into Any Blank Space In-Between the "options" and "script hub" buttons.
4. Enjoy! (Go Click The "UI Themes" Button And Find "Remove Custom Theme" To Remove The Theme.
